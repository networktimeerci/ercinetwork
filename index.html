Overview

ERCI Network (ERCI) is a simple ERC‑20 token deployed on Base. The smart contract uses standard ERC‑20 behavior with a fixed total supply and no special transfer logic (no taxes, no blacklist, no timelocks, no mint function).

ERCI is an independent project and is not affiliated with third‑party issuers or brands.
